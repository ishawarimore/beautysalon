V CUT

cut hair is a type of haircut that is heavily layered at a sharp angle, forming a “V” shaped point at the ends. It’s the option to choose when you’re looking to add a bold dimension and a natural body to your haircut without much effort! A smart choice for ladies with a thick mane, the V-cut hair lets you have as much length as you want without all the weight. Plus, the angular haircut not only frames the face with its layered pieces but also gives the illusion of a slimmer waist. See how it worked for Hollywood fashion icons Jessica Biel, Shay Mitchell, Jennifer Aniston, and Kim Kardashian! Virginia hairstylist Sonny Nguyen finishes his balayage creations with long layers and a V-shape point, and they couldn’t look more phenomenal! Also, having texture is no problem. Shaping wavy hair into this cut results in a loose, cascading layered effect that gives an extra feminine and carefree aura.
